Be at your viewers' mercy!
Let your Twitch Chat or your Discord server control what happens in your Minecraft world. Will they play with you, or against you?
Twitch Vs Minecraft (or "Streamer Vs Chat") is a Minecraft mod for Forge inspired by Kaze Emanuar and CrowdControl that lets Twitch viewers and Discord members interact with the game to provide a fun challenge for streamers.
